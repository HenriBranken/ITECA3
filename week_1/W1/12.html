Original HTML (displayed)


<section>
  <h2>Links (target attribute) Examples</h2>

  <p>
    <strong>_self</strong> — Default.<br />
    Opens the document in the same window/tab:
    <a href="https://example.com" target="_self">Open in same tab (_self)</a>
  </p>

  <p>
    <strong>_blank</strong><br />
    Opens the document in a new window or tab:
    <a
      href="https://example.com"
      target="_blank"
      rel="noopener noreferrer"
    >Open in new tab (_blank)</a>
  </p>

  <p>
    <strong>_parent</strong><br />
    Opens the document in the parent frame:
    <a href="https://example.com" target="_parent">Open in parent frame (_parent)</a>
  </p>

  <p>
    <strong>_top</strong><br />
    Opens the document in the full body of the window:
    <a href="https://example.com" target="_top">Open in full window (_top)</a>
  </p>
</section>
